Working Process at Dumdaddy Pvt. Ltd.

Thank you for considering Dumdaddy for your web design, app development, and digital marketing needs. We have a tried-and-true working process that is designed to ensure that our clients are satisfied with the end result. Below is a step-by-step guide to our process:

Step 1: Initial Consultation
The first step in our process is to schedule an initial consultation with one of our sales/support executives. During this consultation, we will discuss your project requirements and answer any questions you may have. We believe that clear communication is key to a successful project.
Step 2: Understanding Requirements
Once we have a good understanding of your project requirements, we will work with you to define the scope of the project. This will include identifying your target audience, understanding your competition, and determining the results you want to achieve. We want to make sure that we are aligned with your goals from the start.
Step 3: Proposal and Estimate
Based on our understanding of your requirements, we will prepare a draft proposal and estimate for your project. This proposal will outline the scope of the project, the timeline, and the budget. We will send this proposal to you for review and feedback.
Step 4: Contract and Upfront Payment
Once you have reviewed and approved the proposal, we will send you a contract to sign and an invoice for the upfront payment, which is typically 25% of the total project cost. We require this payment to begin work on your project.
Step 5:S Workspace Portal
After we receive your payment, we will create a workspace portal for you. This is a Dumdaddy CRM where you can see the progress of your project, upload files, raise tickets, and schedule meetings with our team. You will receive an email from us with your login details and a password reset link.
Step 6: Wireframe Creation
Wireframe creation is an integral part of website design and app development. Our creative team will work together to prepare a wireframe that is right for your business.
Step 7: Initial Design Concept
Once the wireframe is approved, we will create an initial design concept for your project. This will give you an idea of how the final design will look and feel. We will then ask for your feedback and make any necessary changes.
Step 8: Design Revision
After receiving your feedback on the initial design concept, we will make the necessary revisions. We will ask for your final approval before moving forward.
Step 9: Testing and Delivery
Once the design is finalized, we will put it through multiple testing phases to ensure that it delivers an optimal user experience. After testing is complete, we will deliver the final product to you.
Step 10: Free Maintenance
We believe in providing excellent customer service. We offer 15 days of free maintenance after the delivery of your project to ensure that any issues are promptly taken care .
